About Anirudha Tripathy

Anirudha Tripathy is a seasoned financial strategist and Ex-Vice President with extensive experience transforming financial operations for global organizations. Holding an M.Com and B.Com(H), he combines deep academic foundations with the rigorous discipline of Big 4 consulting. Anirudha’s expertise transcends borders, skillfully navigating complex accounting landscapes across India (IAS/GST), the US (GAAP/Tax), EMEA, Australia, and the Middle East.

He specializes in high-stakes financial modeling, delivering precision in LBOs, PCAP, and waterfall distribution models for private equity. Beyond the numbers, Anirudha is a problem solver, adept at managing GBS setups, optimizing accounting operations, and ensuring strict regulatory compliance. Dedicated to excellence, he empowers clients—from agile startups to multinational corporations—with actionable financial insights and robust operational frameworks that drive sustainable growth.
